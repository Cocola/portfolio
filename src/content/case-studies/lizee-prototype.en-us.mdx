## Context

In 2021, work was underway to build the first version of the Reuse Management System (RMS),
Lizee’s SaaS for circular logistics. To win over investors, the team needed a support that
could faithfully project the product vision, well beyond what the application could show at
that stage.

## The solution

I designed a demo as a carousel inside a modal, through a HiFi Figma prototype. The format
gives a faithful projection of the features to come and provides the founders with a
high-quality presentation support for their investor meetings.
